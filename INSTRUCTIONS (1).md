## Instructions

### App Planning & Requirements Gathering
Clarify the core purpose and audience before building.

- **What is your app called?**
  Digestive Diary

- **What core problem does it solve, and for whom?**
  Helps individuals with digestive or stomach disorders track their symptoms and diet.

- **Can you describe the key workflow or the "main magic" moment for users?**
  Users can easily log their daily food intake and symptoms, receiving personalized insights to manage their condition better.


### Essential Features
- **List the must-have features the app requires for a useful MVP.**
  Food diary, symptom tracker, personalized recommendations, and data analytics.

- **Which features are nice-to-have or for future versions?**
  Integrations with wearable devices, social sharing for support groups, and advanced AI-driven health advice.


### User Experience
- **What are the main user roles?**
  Primary users are individuals with digestive disorders, and secondary users could be healthcare providers for monitoring and advice.

- **What should each user be able to do?**
  Users can track diet, log symptoms, view progress, and receive recommendations. Healthcare providers can monitor patient data and offer guidance.

- **Are there any access restrictions?**
  Access to patient data is restricted to authorized healthcare providers, with user consent.


### Integrations & Notifications
- **Should the app integrate with external APIs or services?**
  Potential integrations include Google Fit, Apple Health, and nutrition databases for accurate food information.

- **For the MVP, do you want real integrations or simulated flows?**
  For the MVP, simulated flows can be used, with real integrations developed in later versions.

- **Should users receive notifications? If yes, how often and for what?**
  Users receive weekly summaries of their progress, reminders to log food and symptoms, and occasional motivational messages.


### Data, Security & Privacy
- **What core data or objects are stored?**
  The app stores user profiles, food diaries, symptom logs, and progress data.

- **Should users have privacy settings or profiles?**
  Users have control over their data privacy, including options to share data with healthcare providers or keep it private.

- **Any GDPR or compliance needs?**
  The app complies with GDPR and relevant health data protection regulations.


### Special Features
- **Should users be able to upload media?**
  Users can upload photos of their meals for easier logging and to track visual progress.

- **Any AI or automation features?**
  AI is used for personalized recommendations based on user data and to predict potential digestive issues.

- **Any unique requirements?**
  A unique feature could be a community forum for users to share experiences and advice, moderated by healthcare professionals.


### Design & Branding
- **Any specific design vision, brand values, colors, or style?**
  A clean, minimalistic design that emphasizes ease of use and accessibility, with a calming color scheme to reduce user stress.

- **Should the app support dark/light mode?**
  The app supports both dark and light modes to accommodate user preferences and reduce eye strain.


### About / Personal Touch
- **Would you like a creator’s note or story section?**
  A creator’s note explaining the purpose and mission of the app, and how it aims to help users manage their digestive health.

- **Anything personal you want in an "About" screen?**
  Information about the development team, including their backgrounds in health and technology, and contact details for feedback and support.
